Why use DefiTrader.app

Easy of USe

  • No need to create wallets for every blockchain.
  • No need to figure out which blockchain, smart contract address or website to use.
  • No need to bridge/transfer funds to different blockchains and/or wallets.
  • Accessible using Website, Discord and Telegram bot.
  • Best trade prices for buy & selling by finding the optimal trading platform for routing.

Secure

  • All cryptocurrencies being traded are checked to reduce the risk of fake tokens, honeypots and rugpulls.
  • Cryptocurrencies are linked to their Smart contract addresses to prevent buying the incorrect or fake tokens with the same name.
  • Protections against Front/backrunning and MEV by using private transactions and slippage calculations.
